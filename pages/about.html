About Us

Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned. Mission-Driven. Results-Focused.

Our Story

SDV Commodity Group LLC was founded in December 2025 in Las Vegas, Nevada, by a service-disabled veteran with a clear mission: to provide federal agencies with reliable, efficient supply chain solutions backed by military-grade discipline and accountability.

As a single-member LLC and verified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), we specialize in sourcing and delivering medical equipment, IT peripherals, and office supplies to government agencies across the United States.

Our founder's military background instilled the values that drive every aspect of our business: precision in execution, integrity in every transaction, and an unwavering commitment to the mission at hand.
